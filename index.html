Vice Chancellor Message

Professor Dr. Md. Matiar Rahman Howlader

From its inception in 2006, Sylhet Agricultural University (SAU) has the mandate to become the flagship university in the arena of agricultural education, research and innovation in Bangladesh and beyond. Thus, it is equally important for us to be close to the interest of our communities within Bangladesh and internationally.
